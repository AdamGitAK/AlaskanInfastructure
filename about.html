Ariel Hasse-Zamudio

Ariel is a lifelong Alaskan who was born and raised in the Mat-Su Valley. She graduated from Caltech ('20) with a BS in Physics before spending time in the D.C. area. Her family recently relocated back to Alaska, where she was the recipiant of the 2025 Chang Career Exploration Prize. This has allowed her to pursue her passion of researching data driven science policy to bring renewable energy to rural Alaska. Ariel also works with various nonprofits in her community and works part-time as a naturalist science guide.
